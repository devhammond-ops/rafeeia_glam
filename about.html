About Me

A journey of beauty, creativity, and connection

Hello! I'm Rafeeia

My name is Rafeeia Awudu, the heart and hands behind ByFeeyahFaces. As the first child in my family, I grew up with a natural love for beauty and creativity. I vividly remember sneaking into my mother’s makeup bag as a little girl, experimenting with lip gloss and powders, and even once shaving off my eyebrows in an attempt to shape them—only to proudly draw them back on with an eyebrow pencil. Even then, I knew beauty was more than a look; it was about confidence and expression.

In high school, makeup became more than just a childhood fascination—it became my passion. I found joy in helping my friends get ready for events, turning entertainment nights into opportunities to create, experiment, and learn. In university, I took a bold step by saving up to order my very first set of professional makeup products from Nigeria, launching what became my first real makeup business.

But like many journeys, mine hasn’t been a straight line. The pressure of school, the uncertainty of the future, and life’s challenges led me to step away from makeup for a while. For three years, I set aside the brushes and palettes I once loved. Still, destiny has a way of calling us back to what we’re meant to do. One evening, a close friend asked me to do her makeup, and the joy I felt in that moment reminded me why I started. That night reignited the passion I thought I had lost, and I promised myself never to let it go again.

Today, ByFeeyahFaces is more than just a makeup brand—it’s a story of resilience, passion, and purpose. Every face I work on is a canvas where I bring out confidence, elegance, and beauty. My dream is to grow ByFeeyah into a global brand that not only transforms faces but also expands into other creative ventures, including fashion and beauty products.

Makeup, to me, is not just about colors and brushes—it’s about helping people see the best version of themselves. And with every stroke, I’m reminded of the little girl who once shaved her eyebrows, laughed at her mistakes, and still believed she looked beautiful. That same fearless spirit guides everything I create today.
